## Summary

### Market gaps ResinRiva fills
- **Luxury art direction is rare.** Most India players are functional/craft-catalogue sites; few present as a *premium studio* with cohesive design, motion and editorial portfolio. This is ResinRiva's core differentiator.
- **No structured on-page customisation.** Competitors say "DM for custom"; ResinRiva offers typed customisation fields (swatch/size/select/file), a **live WhatsApp message preview**, and reference-image upload — captured verbatim as an enquiry.
- **Resin AND 3D printing under one roof.** Most do one or the other. ResinRiva spans resin art, preservation, furniture *and* 3D-printed decor.
- **Thin content/SEO.** Few maintain a real journal with schema markup. ResinRiva ships sitemap, robots, JSON-LD (Organization/Product/FAQ/Article/Breadcrumb) and a 50+ article journal.

### Pricing insights for India (drove the seed price bands)
- Keychains/jewellery: **₹150–1,800** (gift/impulse).
- Trays, clocks, decor: **₹400–7,500** (core gifting).
- Wall art & frames: **₹1,200–26,000** (statement/wedding).
- Varmala preservation: **₹3,500–15,000** (emotional premium; long lead time).
- Furniture (river tables etc.): **bespoke / enquiry** (₹18k–₹140k+ signalled, "Enquire" on-site).
- 3D-printed decor: **₹500–8,000** depending on size/detail.
