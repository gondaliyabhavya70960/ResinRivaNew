# CONTENT_GUIDE.md — Content & Photography

> Status: outline (Phase 1 stub). Expanded in Phases 6–8 and 11.

## Brand voice
Elegant, warm, handcrafted-luxury, immersive. "An heirloom you'll keep forever." Audience: gift-buyers, couples, interior-design lovers, corporates, collectors in India.

## Writing products
- **Title**: evocative + descriptive (e.g. "Ocean Wave Resin Wall Clock").
- **Short tagline**: one luxurious line.
- **Description**: 80–150 words; materials, craft, use, gifting context. 100% original.
- **Customization fields**: size, colour/tint, theme/pattern, engraving text, finish, etc.
- **Price**: INR range or "Enquire" for furniture/large art.
- **SEO**: unique title + description per product.

## Writing blog posts
- 800–1500 words, original, helpful. Topics: resin care guides, customization idea lists, gift guides (wedding/anniversary/housewarming/corporate), resin vs. alternatives, 3D printing explainers (materials, finishes, lithophanes), behind-the-scenes, trend roundups, festival gifting (Diwali, Rakhi, wedding season), FAQ-style.
- Each post: category, tags, excerpt, cover image, SEO title/description, internal links to relevant products, staggered publish dates.

## Photography direction
- Desaturated, cohesive, light-led; resin colour pops against ivory/ink.
- Macro gloss + depth shots; styled-in-luxe-interior lifestyle shots; generous whitespace.
- Product set: square 1:1 main + macro detail + lifestyle 4:5 / 16:9 + scale-in-hand shot + hover second image.
- Formats: WebP, 2000px+ for product, 2400px for hero/parallax. Blog images 1600×900.

## Video
- Hero ≤6MB, 10–20s loop with poster; clips 8–15s ≤8MB. MP4 (H.264) + WebM. Keep web-optimized.

## Placeholders
- Unsplash/Pexels during build ("resin art", "epoxy river table", "ocean resin", "luxury interior"). Tag placeholder alt text clearly. Replace ALL placeholders with real ResinRiva photos before launch. AI generation OK only for abstract texture/background plates — never for real product shots.

## Originality rule
Competitors inform TYPES/topics only. Never copy text, names, descriptions, or images.
